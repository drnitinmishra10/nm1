Artificial Intelligence in Effective Diagnosis of Diseases

Applications of AI in Disease Diagnosis

Sr. No. Disease / Area How AI Helps in Diagnosis
1 Cancer Diagnosis AI analyzes CT, MRI, PET, ultrasound, and pathology images for early cancer detection and tumor classification.
2 Diabetic Retinopathy AI screens retinal images to detect eye damage caused by diabetes.
3 Breast Cancer AI supports mammography and ultrasound-based tumor detection.
4 Lung Diseases AI detects pneumonia, tuberculosis, COVID-19, and lung cancer using X-ray and CT images.
5 Heart Diseases AI helps in ECG analysis, cardiac image interpretation, and cardiovascular risk prediction.
6 Parkinson’s Disease AI uses voice, gait, motor symptoms, and image data for early detection.
7 Mental Health Disorders AI assists in diagnosis, monitoring, and risk prediction for depression and anxiety disorders.
8 Sepsis Detection AI predicts sepsis risk using patient vitals, lab results, and electronic health records.
9 Retinal Diseases AI helps diagnose macular degeneration and other retinal disorders.
10 Neurological Disorders AI supports diagnosis of Alzheimer’s disease, stroke, epilepsy, and brain tumors.
11 Skin Diseases AI classifies skin lesions and helps detect melanoma through image analysis.
12 Infectious Diseases AI supports diagnosis and outbreak prediction for influenza, Ebola, tuberculosis, and COVID-19.
13 Gastrointestinal Diseases AI helps detect colon cancer and polyps during endoscopy.
14 Kidney Diseases AI predicts chronic kidney disease and kidney failure risk using clinical data.
15 Liver Diseases AI assists in diagnosing fatty liver, hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer.
16 Oral and Dental Diseases AI detects dental caries, oral cancer, and jaw abnormalities from dental images.
17 Pediatric Diseases AI supports early diagnosis in children using imaging and clinical records.
18 Rare Diseases AI identifies hidden patterns in genetic and clinical data for faster diagnosis.
19 Clinical Laboratory Diagnosis AI improves interpretation of blood tests and laboratory reports.
20 Personalized Disease Prediction AI predicts disease risk, progression, complications, and treatment response.
